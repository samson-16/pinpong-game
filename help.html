How to Play Ping Pong

Objective:

The goal of the game is to score points by hitting the ball past your opponent's paddle.

Play Against the Computer:

Control: Use the computer mouse to move the paddle up and down.

Local Multiplayer:

For Left Player: Use the mouse to move the paddle up and down.

For Right Player: Use Arrow Up and Arrow Down keys to move up and down, respectively.

Winning the Game:

The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.
